ABOUT US

DRIVE-BY BEST AUTO DETAILING SERVICE

DRIVE-BY is an auto detailing centre that opened in 2022. Providing a wide range of services related to vehicle astatic maintenance. We offer car washing, deep cleaning both inside and out, a full car spa, and other detailing services. We also offer steam cleaning, ceramic coating, and PPF work.

I have an engineering degree, a master's degree in business administration, and more than 15 years of experience working for renowned companies including CBRE, Unilever, FIAT Chrysler Automobiles, and Tata Motors.

I've always had a love for automobiles. I have loved vehicles ever since I was a young child because I have grown up around them. When I purchased my first car in my late teens, I developed a passion for them and became fixated on keeping them clean. I soon understood that I needed to turn my passion into a business. I made the decision to open my own auto detailing studio at that point. Since then, we have expanded.

Our goal is to establish a brand that can offer a variety of services under one roof. with exceptional products and services that provide the ideal detailing experience.
