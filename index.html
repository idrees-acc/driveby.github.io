FULL AUTO DETAILING

If put simply, car detailing refers to cleaning and protecting your car from top to bottom using various tools and different techniques which are usually not used in the traditional car cleaning or car washing methods. Car detailing involves a few cosmetic touch-ups here and there to repair your car's paintwork. What is included in a full detail? But what exactly does a full detail entail? Well, just like it sounds, a full detail covers all non-mechanical components of your vehicle: Tyres and wheels, exterior paint, chrome trim, the interior surfaces, seats and carpets. It's a head-to-toe revitalisation for your vehicle. The amount of time required to detail a car will depend on the detailing services you choose. A quick interior detail might take two hours, while a comprehensive exterior detail might take two days. If you wonder why detailing time can vary, it depends on what needs to get done. The frequency that you should get your car details varies by how much wear and tear you put on your vehicle and how often you wash and wax it, but most experts recommend you have it done 2 to 3 times a year, or once every 4 to 6 months

CERAMIC & GRAPHENE COATING

Advanced coatings derived with nano-technology that help prevent environmental damages on your car's precious paint. These coatings form a 9H or 10H hard crystallised layer on top of your vehicle's clear coat and provide resistance against light scratches, chemical / acids and also protects the paint against harmful UV rays. You will also experience hydrophobic properties with the application of these coatings, which would avoid water markings to a great extent. These coatings are available with warranty plans.

PAINT PROTECTION FILM

Famously known as PPF, this transparent high-gloss film is the most advanced and rugged protection that you can offer your vehicle. Afraid of being keyed by the neighbours kids, or just worried that your better half might scratch the bumper in the parking? Well PPF will help prevent almost all deep scratches and gashes with its thick layer of protection. PPF which completely heals all swirl marks or superficial scratches that appear due to improper washing. PPF prevents almost every possible scratch from reaching the paint. Paint protection films come with warranty plans.

OZONE AIR PURIFYING TREATMENT

It effectively removes smoke, dust, and bad smell. It purifies the interior of a vehicle by breaking down odors caused by pollutants at their source, leaving the vehicle smelling fresh and clean. Once the source of the odor is eliminated, the odors won't return and there are no remaining artificial fragrances. Ozone treatment is the use of the gas ozone (O3) to remove odors, bacteria, and viruses. We will place an ozone generator in your vehicle for a designated amount of time, depending on the strength of the odor. As the gas permeates your car, the odor is neutralized and any bacteria and viruses are killed.
